Match Criteria

More information can be found on the Help Page. Short summary below.

Basic boolean operators:

  • AND: fender & rednef -or- fender rednef
  • OR : fender | rednef
  • NOT: rednef -fender -or- rednef !fender
  • Grouping: fox & (box | socks)

Capabilities

  • Field searching: @title fender @message rednef
  • Phrase searching: "quick brown fox"
  • Word proximity searching: "quick brown fox"~3
  • Quorum matching: "red_fox gray_fox arctic_fox fennec swift_fox kit_fox"/2

Available Fields

  • @title
  • @message
  • @lower (artist name as it appears in their userpage URL)
  • @keywords
Example:
(fender | rednef) -truck @keywords plushie
